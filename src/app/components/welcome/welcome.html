Why does your back, neck, or head hurt?

Most chronic pain doesn’t come from joints or nerves. It comes from trigger points — tight, irritated muscle fibers that refer pain to other areas of the body. Trigger Hub helps you find and treat the real source.

What Are Trigger Points?

Trigger points are hypersensitive spots inside your muscles. They create pain, stiffness, tension, and referred symptoms in other areas of the body. Most people have them — they just don’t know the cause.
