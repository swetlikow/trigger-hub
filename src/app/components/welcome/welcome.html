Why Does Your Body Hurt?

Most people think pain comes from nerves, joints, or bones. In reality, a huge amount of chronic discomfort comes from trigger points — small, tight knots in your muscles that cause pain in other areas of your body.

Tight spot in your neck → headache.
Tight muscle in your lower back → hip or leg pain.
Tight chest muscle → upper back tension.

You feel the pain — but the real source is hidden. Trigger Hub helps you find it.

What Are Trigger Points?

Trigger points are tight knots inside your muscles. They form when fibers become overloaded, strained, stressed, or not allowed to recover. A trigger point can cause pain right where it is — or send pain far away (this is called referred pain).

The good news? Trigger points are treatable, and most people can learn to manage them with simple techniques.
